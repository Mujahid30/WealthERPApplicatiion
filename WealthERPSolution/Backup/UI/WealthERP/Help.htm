ADVISOR

New advisor Registration

  • Click on ‘Register Here’ link on the WERP main page.
  • Choose ‘Register as an advisor’ on the Registration Type and click on ‘submit’ button
  • Fill in the details and click ‘Next’
  • A username and password will be generated.  Click on login to go to the login page.
  • Enter your username and password to enter into WERP as an advisor.
  • It will prompt you to change your password. Enter the current, new and confirm password fields and click on submit.
  • This time enter the username and the NEW password to login.
Points to Note in this page:
  • Fields marked as * are mandatory.
  • If you are a multibranch network, Choose ‘Yes’ on Multibranch Network in Contacts Details.
  • Choosing ‘MF’ or ‘Equity’ in LOB will take you to the LOB details page wherein you will have to enter your ARN code or NSE/BSE registration number. (This step is optional. You can also add LOBs later).
  • You can also upload your organization logo by clicking on the ‘browse’ button and selecting an image file. Files supported are jpg, bmp, png.

RM

Add a new RM

  1. Click on + sign next to ‘RM’ under Advisor Home in the left pane. After it is collapsed, Click on ‘Add RM’.
  2. Enter the required fields and click on ‘Submit’ button. The RM is displayed in the RM grid.
Points to Note in this page:
  1. Fields marked as * are mandatory.
  2. If the RM is working under a branch, and the branch is already setup, the branch details will reflected in this page. We can pick and choose the branch to be associated to the RM.
  3. If Branch Setup is done, then a list of branches will be displayed in this page. You can associate an RM under a branch by picking the branch and choosing ‘is main branch’.
  4. If Branch Setup is not done, then the list is empty.

BRANCH

Add a new Branch

  1. Click on + sign next to Branch under Advisor Home in the left pane. After it is collapsed, Click on ‘Add Branch’.
  2. Enter the required fields and click on ‘Submit’ button. The Branch will be displayed in the Branch grid.
Points to Note in this page:
  1. Fields marked as * are mandatory.
  2. Before setting a Branch, the Branch Head needs to be setup as an RM under the advisor.

Customer

Add Customer

PS: Only an RM can add a customer for now.
  1. Click on ‘Switch Roles’ on the left pane. Select RM to go on RM Dashboard.
  2. Click on + sign next to Customers and click on ‘Add customer’.
  3. Select the Type of Customer you want to enter, fill in the basic details and click on ‘Submit’ button. To enter more details, Click on ‘Detail Customer Profile’.
  4. Enter the required information and click on ‘Submit’ button to view the customer grid.
